About US

Why Parmeshwar ?

Parmeshwar Hospital is committed to a patient-centric approach and clinical excellence and is a one-stop destination for best-in-class multispecialty healthcare services. It is a part of Parmeshwar Hospitals Group, which has a presence in 13 cities spanning 8 states with 12 multispecialty and 4 Parmeshwar Orthopedics Centres of Excellence. Parmeshwar is the largest chain of multispecialty hospitals in Western and Central India. It was founded by Dr. Vikram Shah, a world-renowned joint replacement surgeon and is today the largest joint replacement centre of the world. It performs more than 14,000 joint replacement surgeries a year and in the last 30 years has performed more than 1,45,000 joint replacements.
